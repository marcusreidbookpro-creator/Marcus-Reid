How It Works

Six Stages. Full Documentation. Zero Confusion.

Before you invest in any book marketing service, you deserve to know exactly how your project will be managed, what will be delivered, and how results will be measured and reported.

Stage 01

Initial Inquiry

Your project begins with one email. Send your project inquiry with your book details, current marketing situation, specific goals, timeline, and investment budget range.

  • Your inquiry is reviewed personally by Marcus Reid
  • A detailed, personalized response is prepared
  • Response delivered within 24 to 48 hours
Stage 02

Strategy Proposal

You receive a custom-written marketing proposal, not a generic brochure. Includes detailed assessment, service recommendations, campaign approach, timeline, and investment details.

  • Fully documented proposal in writing
  • Everything discussed before engagement begins
  • Delivered within 24 to 48 hours of inquiry
Stage 03

Agreement and Onboarding

A formal written service agreement is sent via email detailing every service, deliverable, timeline, and responsibilities of both parties. Campaign officially begins.

  • Written service agreement, no exceptions
  • Onboarding document provided
  • All materials submitted via email
Stage 04

Campaign Execution

Your strategy is executed exactly as documented with precision, professionalism, and complete transparency. No changes without written communication and confirmation.

  • Every element implemented per written strategy
  • Adjustments communicated before implementation
  • All activities documented and reportable
Stage 05

Progress Reporting

Regular written progress reports delivered on defined schedule covering every key metric, campaign activity, and performance indicator. Full transparency at all times.

  • Weekly or bi-weekly written reports
  • Real performance data and analysis
  • Strategic notes and documented adjustments
Stage 06

Final Report and Review

A comprehensive written campaign report documenting everything built, executed, and achieved. Your complete record to reference and build on for future decisions.

  • Full campaign summary start to finish
  • Comprehensive performance data
  • Strategic recommendations for continued growth
